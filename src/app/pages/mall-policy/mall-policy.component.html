Buying Information

Trenderz Hub:

Trenderz Hub is here for the Edmonton and Area community to help bring them together in supporting our local merchants. If you would like to support our local merchants for the Edmonton area this is an easy way to do so. Shopping online, can be done anytime in the comfort of your own home, all the while knowing that you are helping our local economy.

Becoming a member:
You may browse through the items without becoming a member, and you may buy from a merchant without becoming a member. You can browse stores anytime, by clicking the “Browse Stores” button. If you choose to become a member by creating an account, you have the advantage of things like auto-fill for your shipping address, and wishlist items that are saved for you even when you log-out.

Becoming a member is easy and free. You simply click on- My Account- located in the top right hand corner of the Home Page. Choose "become a member". Fill out name, email, and create password. Do not share your password, and keep it secure at all times. Click the "Submit" button at the botom of the page. You will be taken to "Your Dashboard" page and from here you can set your default shipping address and even add multiple ones if you like. Shipping information must be accurate and current.

Purchasing procedures:
If you would like to make a purchase, you will be routed through Pay Pal. You don't have to be a member of PayPal to purchase and item, but it easier if you do, especially if you want to make more than one purchase. On the PayPal site, they have all the information on how Pay Pal works if you are unfamiliar with the site. When you are routed through PayPal on Trenderz Hub, you will be taken directly to the true PayPal site.


You may put items from several different stores in your “shopping cart”, but must complete each transaction within each store as each merchant is responsible for their own store, and store policies. This includes shipping policies. We encourage you to look into each store policy, to see if their policies are ones that suit your needs. Please remember to be safe at all times. If you do agree to pick up an item you have purchased, please do so in a safe and secure manner.

We hope that you enjoy your shopping experience. Should you have any questions or comments, click "Contact Us" at the footer of any page.

Happy Shopping!!

Selling Information

Trenderz Hub:

Trenderz Hub is here for the Edmonton and area community to help bring them together in supporting our local merchants. If you want to sell, it is an easy way start your new business or add to your current business at afforadable prices. Trenderz Hub has four packages for you to choose from. --Bronze package: $15.00 to display up to 5 items at a time. --Silver package: $25.00 to display up to 15 items at a time. --Gold package: $35.00 to display up to 35 items at a time. --Platinum package: $50.00 to display up to 100 items at a time. These package fees are renewable 12 months from the time of purchase. With each package, you can switch out your products to replace with a new product at any time, as long as you honor your last sale of the item listed. Trenderz Hub receives a 3% commision on the sale of an item. The Pay Pal fee is extra. You can see the Pay Pal fees on their website.

Basic Needs:

If you would like to open a store on Trenderz Hub, you need to be at least 18 years of age. You will also need to become a member of Trenderz Hub, and set up a PayPal account through PayPal. If you already have a PayPal account, you can still use it for this site. The PayPal account is needed for you to open your store, list your items, and it is how you will get paid for your sales. In order to set up a PayPal account you must adhear to the rules and regulations set out by PayPal. PayPal is a secure site for which to conduct business. Please make sure that you are always dealing with the true PayPal site.

“When you receive a PayPal email, always:

  • Make sure that it comes from @paypal.com.
  • Make sure that the address uses your first and last name or business name.
  • Ignore any request for your login or password.

When in doubt, type www.paypal.com in your browser instead of clicking email links. Please forward suspicious emails to spoof@paypal.com”

The above statement is directly from the PayPal site.

You should be a locally based merchant from Edmonton or surrounding area within aproximately a one hundred mile radius. If you are not sure if you are in this area, please email Trenderz Hub to ask if you are considered local for the Edmonton area. When you open a store, you will want to have a list of policies in place for your store, such as your return policy, shipping ploicies, wheather taxes are included, extra, or not needed (please check G.S.T. Laws, and govern yourself accordingly). You will want to decided on this before opening your store, so that you can write these policies out for your customers to read. You may update these policies anytime.

Becoming a member:

You become a member by clicking on the “become a member” button. It costs nothing to become a member. This button is available for both merchants and consumers, but you need to become a member if you wish to open a store. You will create a log-in name, and a password, which is for your own personal use. Do not share your password, and keep it secure at all times. You then fill out your personal information which must be accurate and current. We do not share your personal information.

Opening a store:

After becoming a member and you want to open a store to become a merchant on the website, you now can click the “open a store” button beside the “browse stores” button. “Terms and Conditions” pop up and you then click “I agree” if these terms are acceptable to you. If they are not acceptable to you, and you click “I disagree”, you will not be able to open a store. If you have a question that you would like answered before deciding if you are comfortable opening a store, please email Trenderz Hub.

It will cost $25.00 to open your store. You do have a trial period of 90 days, for which to try out your store on this site. If you do not wish to keep the store and want to close it within this intial 90 day period, let us know by email, and we will refund this cost. Anytime after the 90 day trial period, the fee of $25.00 is not refundable. You may not re-open the same store under the same name once you have decided to close. You may, however shut down your shop for up to 190 days (about 6 months) if you wanted to take a little time off for any reason such as a vacation, sickness or unforseen circumstances. In such a case just email Trenderz Hub to let us know that you would like to do this, and we will put up a “temporaily closed” sign up for you. After 190 days time, if we have not heard from you, we will close the shop permanently.

How to list your items:

Once you have become a member, and opened your store, you may now list your items. You click the “list item” button, and you then upload the pictures of your item(s) along with your description and price. Pictures can be uploaded with almost any camera that you would normally use, such as a smartphone or digital camera. Image size specifications would come up if it isn't compatable with our site. You may upload as many as 4 pictures of each item to show different angles or colours of the item. Once you feel that it is acceptable to display to potential customers fill in the description and review the description to ensure you are satisfied with the way that the product is represented to the public. Fill in the price that you wish to sell the item for. You may list as many products as you like. When you are finished listing, you pay for the listing fees through PayPal, and your products are now on display, and can be seen by the general public.

The listing fee is 2% per item listed to display your products. For example: If you would like to sell your product for $10.00 the listing fee will cost you 20 cents. The listing fee of 2% per item listed are not refundable once you pay them unless there are special circumstances that Trenderz Hub agrees to (such as: you listed your item for $795.00 when the item was supposed to be listed for $7.95). In this instance, you would need to email Trenderz Hub to request a refund of the 2% listing fee. Both the opening of your store, and the listing of your items are transactions between you and Trenderz Hub. You can add or change your listing items at anytime. We do not accept any item listed for under $5.00. If you have items that you would like to sell for under the $5.00, we suggest that you sell them in a bundle so as to reach the $5.00 minimum.

When deciding on a price to sell your item(s) we know that you will want to make money in your business, so it is wise to cover all your costs and still make a profit without pricing so high that you discourage sales. It is a good idea to always keep this in mind when setting your listing price.

Selling your items;

As an entrepreneur, you will want to create, and continue your service in a responsible manner befitting of any professional store. This is your own shop, and it is in your best interest to treat your customers in a professional manner. If we at Trenderz Hub feel that your business is not conducted in a manner befitting to the standards of Trenderz Hub, we reserve the right to close the store. This includes if it violates any laws or conditions set out in “Terms and Conditions”, or for any other reason at our discretion. If Trenderz Hub decides to close your store, the intital fee of $25.00 will not be refunded. We do however, want you to succeed, because your success is our success.

When a customer purchases an item from your store, they will go through the check-out process, and purchase the item through PayPal. Trenderz Hub takes a 3% commission on this sale at this time when the customer buys the item. PayPal will also take their commission at this time as well. You will be alerted to the sale, and then it is up to you to fill your end of the agreement regarding the purchase as stated in your store policies. Whether you ship, meet up, or have pick-up only, congratulations on your sale! We hope that all parties are happy. However, in the event that the item is needed to be returned to you and you need to refund the customer, Trenderz Hub will return the commission (3%) that we took from the sale of the item. If it looks like there are too many refunds for your store, Trenderz Hub will investigate, and if we feel that your store is not up to what we regard as professional standards, we will close the store.

If you are selling edible items, you must do so in accordance with Alberta Law, and must provide proof that you are liscensed to do so. There is a box for you to check off when you are opening a store, to let us know that you would like to sell edible items. This will take you to a section for you to provide the proof that you need to sell these items. You may want to have the items for pickup only or if you do wish to deliver, you must do so in accordance with Alberta law governing transportation of such items. You will not sell edible items for the purpose of consumption unless you are authorized by the Alberta Government. These items must be stored in a manner that is acceptable to the standards set out by the Alberta Government.

Trenderz Hub will be promoting in the Edmonton area to help ensure traffic to our website, we can also offer business cards to you with the Trenderz Hub website information on one side of the business card which includes a QR code, and on the other side, you can have your store name to direct traffic to your store specifically. Please email us for information if you would like to order some Trenderz Hub business cards.
